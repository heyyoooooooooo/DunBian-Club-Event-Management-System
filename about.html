Our Mission

We strive to promote the art of debate, create an environment where students can learn, practice and understand the different aspects of debating through different engaging activities.

Our Story

The Secretariat of Chinese Debate UTHM which is commonly known as the DunBian Club is a student-led organization which focused on enhancing studentsâ€™ debating and critical thinking skills. The club established in 2017 and registered under the Pejabat Hal Ehwal Pelajar (HEPA) at Universiti Tun Hussein Onn Malaysia (UTHM) but is not affiliated with the Pusat Pengajian Umum dan Kokurikulum (PPUK). .

Activities

We have organised structured debate competitions, engaging activities and gatherings like movie nights and board game sessions.
